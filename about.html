About Electric Picnic

Here is the the About section, in this section you will find information relating to the acts performing, facilites and rules. Electric Picnic is an annual music festival at Stradbally Hall in Co.Laois, that runs from the 30th of August and finishes on the 1st of September. Here you can see some of the hottest acts of the year and enjoy a great time with a freindly atmospehere and great buzz here at EP! Be sure to checl out the other sections of the site to find out more about EP and hope to see you soon at Electric Picnc from 30th of August to the 1st of September!
