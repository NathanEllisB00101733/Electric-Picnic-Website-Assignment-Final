About: Rules

On this page are the rules for the event. These rules are in place for attendees so everyone can enjoy EP! Failure to comply with these rules will lead to warnings, bans or in extreme cases involvement from the Gardai.


  • You must be 18 or over to enter EP, anyone under the age of 18 will not be allowed to enter.

  • You must bring identification to prove your age to enter the festival. A passport, Drving license and Garda age cards will suffice.

  • There are certain items not allowed inside the festival grounds at all, supicious substances or drugs will not be allowed.

  • only a minimum amount of alcohol for personal consumption is allowed.

  • weaponary, lasers, knives and sharp objects arent allowed.

  • Unregistered animals arent allowed, only guide dogs are permited.

  • Vehicles like bikes should be parked outside, scooters and skateboards arent allowed.

  • There is zero tolerence for harassment of other atendees or staff, whether it be physical, verbal, racial or sexual harassment.

  • If any rules are broken, attendee(s) will be given a warning or in extreme cases be asked to leave or the Gardai will be contacted.

  • Please ensure to follow these rules so all attendees, staff and acts can enjoy a safe and positive environment.
